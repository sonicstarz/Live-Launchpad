Privacy Policy

Live Launchpad is an independent, hobby spaceflight project. We try to collect as little about you as possible, and we never sell your data. Here's the plain-English version of what we do and don't do.

Last updated: 4 June 2026

What we collect

Email address — only if you give it to us

If you sign up for launch alerts, we store the email address you enter so we can send you a heads-up before major launches and the occasional dispatch. That's it. We don't require a name. You can unsubscribe at any time, and you can ask us to delete your address (see Contact).

Arcade leaderboard

If you submit a high score, we store the 1–3 character initials you type and your score. These appear publicly on the leaderboard. We don't ask for, or attach, any personal information to a score.

Learning Center account (optional)

If you create an account to track your course progress, we store your email/login and which lessons you've watched and your notes, so the site can show your progress when you return. You can ask us to delete your account and its data.

Your camera stays on your device

🔒 The optional Gesture Mode in the 3D Rocket Explorer uses your webcam to track hand movements. The video is processed entirely inside your own browser — it is never uploaded, recorded, or sent to us or anyone else. Turn it off and the camera stops immediately.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell, rent, or trade your information.
  • We don't run advertising or cross-site ad-tracking.
  • We don't try to build a profile of you across the web.

Third-party services

The site is built on a few well-known services and data sources. When you use the site, your browser talks to some of them directly, and they have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — stores email signups, scores, and Learning Center data.
  • Netlify — hosts the site and serves the pages.
  • The Space Devs — live launch data (Launch Library 2) and news (Spaceflight News API).
  • YouTube — the Videos section and Learning Center embed YouTube players; YouTube may set its own cookies when you play a video.
  • Google Fonts and public code CDNs (jsDelivr, Cloudflare) — serve fonts and libraries; your browser requests these by IP as normal.

Cookies & storage

We don't use advertising cookies. The site uses your browser's local storage for practical things like remembering your game session or keeping you logged in to the Learning Center. Embedded YouTube players may set their own cookies, which are governed by Google's policy.

Children

The site is general-audience and educational. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted their email, contact us and we'll remove it.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time.
  • Delete your data — email us and we'll remove your address, account, or scores.
  • Don't enable the camera — Gesture Mode is entirely optional; mouse and touch always work.

Contact

Questions, or want your data removed? Email hello@livelaunchpad.com.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll post the new version here and change the "last updated" date above. This is a plain-language summary of our practices, not legal advice.
