Gesture Mode

Fly the rocket with your hands

Wave, fling, and pinch in front of your webcam. Here's the playbook:

🖐️
Orbitmove one open hand — fling it and it keeps spinning
✋✋
Zoomtwo hands — apart to pull out, together to push in
☝️🤏
Selectpoint a finger, then pinch to open a part
  • 💡 Bright, even lighting on your hands works best
  • 🖥️ Designed for desktop/laptop with a webcam
  • 📏 Keep hands about an arm's length from the camera
📱 Heads up — you're on a small screen. Gesture Mode really shines on a desktop with a webcam (especially two-hand zoom). You're welcome to try it here anyway.
🔒 The camera runs entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded or stored.
