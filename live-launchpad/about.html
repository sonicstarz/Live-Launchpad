About Live Launchpad

Mission control for the orbital era — one place for live launches, decades of reliability data, breaking news, and a rocket-fueled arcade.

What is this?

Live Launchpad is an independent spaceflight dashboard built by a space enthusiast. It pulls together the things worth tracking about the modern launch era and presents them without the clutter — mission-control style.

  • ▶The Index — reliability scores for every major launch provider, plus the full arc of the Space Age from Sputnik to today.
  • ▶Launch Schedule — live upcoming launches with countdowns and status, from the Launch Library 2 database.
  • ▶News — the latest spaceflight headlines aggregated from across the industry.
  • ▶In Development — the rockets and programs being built right now, with their next milestones.
  • ▶The Arcade — original rocket-themed mini games. Because why not.
